Flow

From website visitor to booked client.

Premium builds are designed around a simple journey, not just pretty pages: landing → reading about your offer → checking out → picking a time → getting reminders.

1

Program page

The visitor lands on an overview or program page (like the main Premium overview) and sees a clear headline, price, and benefits.

2

Checkout

A Stripe-hosted or embedded checkout handles payment securely — credit card, Apple Pay, etc. Your site doesn’t touch card details.

3

Scheduling

After checkout, the client is sent straight to your booking page (Calendly, SavvyCal, Acuity) to pick a time and get automatic reminders.
