Our Story

Moon Tim's began as a roadside grill shack lit only by lanterns and a dream. Today, we still cook with the same passion and flame — just with better lighting.

From the moon-marked ribeye to our golden crust signature filet, every plate is crafted with tradition and fire-kissed flavor.
