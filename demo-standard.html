Our story

Built from backyard cookouts and late-night ideas.

Oak & Ember started as a backyard smoker, beat-up picnic tables, and a whole lot of “you should open a restaurant.” We finally did—with all the smoke and none of the folding chairs.

Kitchen

Fire at the center

From ribeye to carrots, most things spend time over oak. Expect char, smoke, and a little bit of Texas in every plate.
